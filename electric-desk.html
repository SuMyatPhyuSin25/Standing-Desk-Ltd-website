The Recken Desk


Fully Featured Electric Height-Adjustable Desk

Recken meaning: To Stretch Out

The Recken desk is an advanced version of our popular Oben desk. It comes with two strong, quiet motors with advanced collision detection capable of lifting up to 250 lbs. 3 save positions preset buttons let you find your ideal sitting or standing setup. The adjustable frame fits table tops up to 72″. The electric height-adjustable Recken desk frame is an evolution of our popular Oben desk. It comes equipped with two powerful, and quiet electric motors featuring an intelligent collision detection system. With three save preset buttons, you have the option of saving 3 individual positions to find your perfect fit, whether sitting or standing. The Recken also features a variable table top frame ranging from 43.3” – 59”, and fits table tops up to a maximum width of 70”.
